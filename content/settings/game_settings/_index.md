These settings control three things that actually decide fights: how clean your inputs are, how stable your vision is, and how much information you have.

We are stripping out anything that interferes with aim or awareness, and keeping anything that gives us clean data and control. Think of it like tuning a flight control system… you remove noise, reduce latency, and maximize signal clarity.
