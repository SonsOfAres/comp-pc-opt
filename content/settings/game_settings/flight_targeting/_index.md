## Settings Breakdown

### Locked Show (Heading/Distance/Name) — Yes (to all)
- Show Heading  
- Show Distance  
- Show Name  
- Show Reticle  

All the locked target info stays on. Always.

You need all of this in fights.

### Targeting Direction — View Direction
- Uses head tracking if available.  
- Falls back to crosshair if you don't have headtracking.  
- There is no downside to this setting.

### Target Arrow Display Mode — Based on Head
- Keeps directional awareness tied to your view.  
- Helps you track targets off screen more naturally.

### Pinning Overflow Behavior — Irrelevant
- Target pinning is unreliable due to chaff and bugs.
- Pins can't be shared with party.
- Pins can't be shared with team.
- Pins can't be shared with gunners.

### Enable Auto Zoom On Locked Target — No
- Zoom reduces your field of view.  
- Kills situational awareness instantly.
