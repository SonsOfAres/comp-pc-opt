## Settings Breakdown

### VJoy Visibility — Always Visible (kbm) Optional (Joy).
- Gives a visual representation of your inputs.
- Extremely useful for reviewing fights and improving.

Seeing your inputs makes it way easier to debug your own flying or review fights later.

### VJoy Show Deadzone For Mouse — Yes
- Lets you see exactly where your deadzone is.
- Helps with tuning and consistency.

### VJoy Pilots Range (Degrees) — Optional (Mine is 9.00)
- This is visual only.
- It changes the size of the VJoy indicator, not your sensitivity.
- It does not affect input at all.

And just to be really clear, range is visual only. It does not change sensitivity. At all.

### Pilot - VJoy - Mouse Deadzone — As close to `0` as you can
- Keep this as low as possible for responsiveness.  
- It makes the ship harder to “settle,” but gives you much finer control.

Lower deadzone means faster response. It also means you need better control, but that’s kind of the point. Precision over comfort.
